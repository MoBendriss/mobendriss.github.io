Publications Scientifiques

On $p(x)$-Laplacian elliptic equations with singular terms and nonlinear boundary conditions

M. Bendriss, A. El Amrani

Journal of Mathematical Analysis and Applications (2025), Vol. 532, 127980.

Analyse EDP Sobolev non-standard DOI: 10.1016/j.jmaa.2024.127980
arXiv Preprint
This paper establishes the existence and multiplicity of weak solutions for a class of non-local boundary value problems involving the $p(x)$-Laplacian operator with highly singular nonlinearities. By using variational methods and the theory of variable exponent Sobolev spaces, we prove the existence of at least two non-trivial solutions.

Asymptotic behavior of solutions for fractional Laplacian operator on bounded domains

M. Bendriss, K. Oudghiri

Nonlinear Differential Equations and Applications (NoDEA) (2023), Vol. 30, Art. 45.

Fractional Calculus Asymptotic Analysis
DOI Link
We study the asymptotic behavior of weak solutions for fractional boundary value problems of elliptic type. Applying critical point theory combined with structural topological methods, we establish the convergence rates of solutions when the fractional parameter approaches 1.
